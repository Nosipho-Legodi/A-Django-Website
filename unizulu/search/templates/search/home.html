The UNIZULU course finder is a designed for prospective students to equip them with information about courses offered by the University of Zululand. This web App helps them know the requirements needed by these courses, together with the careers that one can go for should they they get that qualification. It also helps these prospective students know which course they qualify for.
NB: You need a minimum of 26 APS to be admitted at for a Bachelor and a minimum of 24 APS to be admitted for National Diploma and higher certificate
